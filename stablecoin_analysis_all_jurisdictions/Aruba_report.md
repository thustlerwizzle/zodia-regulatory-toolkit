**Recommendation:** Develop a comprehensive Reserve Management & Custody Policy mandating 100% HQLA backing in segregated accounts. Establish contracts with qualified custodians and a schedule for monthly attestations and annual audits by a registered public accountant.

**Impact:** High risk of facilitating financial crime, leading to severe regulatory penalties, loss of banking relationships, and reputational destruction.
