## Critical Priority Gaps

### Gap 1: GAP-001
**Requirement:** Stablecoin issuers must maintain 1:1 reserves in high-quality liquid assets (typically cash or cash equivalents) to back all issued stablecoins.

**Current Status:** No documented policy on reserve requirements or asset backing.

**Gap:** Complete absence of reserve management framework, custody arrangements, or asset verification procedures.

**Impact:** Risk of regulatory enforcement, loss of consumer trust, potential insolvency if redemption demands exceed reserves, and legal liability for misrepresentation.

**Recommendation:** Develop and implement a comprehensive Reserve Management Policy specifying: 1) Acceptable reserve assets (e.g., cash, short-term government securities), 2) Custody arrangements with qualified custodians, 3) Monthly independent attestations or audits of reserves, 4) Public disclosure of reserve composition.

### Gap 2: GAP-002
**Requirement:** Stablecoin issuers must implement robust Anti-Money Laundering (AML), Counter-Terrorist Financing (CTF), and Know Your Customer (KYC) programs for all transactions and wallet holders.

**Current Status:** No AML/CTF or KYC policy documented.

**Gap:** No framework for customer identification, transaction monitoring, suspicious activity reporting, or sanctions screening.

**Impact:** Severe risk of facilitating illicit finance, leading to regulatory sanctions, fines, loss of banking relationships, and criminal prosecution.

**Recommendation:** Immediately draft and adopt a Financial Crime Compliance Program. This must include: 1) Risk-based KYC/CDD procedures for all users, 2) Transaction monitoring systems, 3) Procedures for filing Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs), 4) Regular employee training, and 5) Appointment of a designated Compliance Officer.

## High Priority Gaps

### Gap 1: GAP-003
**Requirement:** Issuers must provide clear, accurate, and regular disclosures to the public and regulators regarding the stablecoin's operation, risks, and reserve status.

**Recommendation:** Create a Transparency and Disclosure Policy mandating: 1) Publicly accessible terms of service outlining redemption rights and risks, 2) Monthly publication of reserve attestation reports, 3) Clear communication of the stablecoin's governance and operational mechanics.

### Gap 2: GAP-004
**Requirement:** A secure and reliable redemption mechanism must be in place, allowing holders to redeem stablecoins for the referenced fiat currency at par value within a reasonable timeframe.

**Recommendation:** Establish a formal Redemption Process Policy detailing: 1) User verification steps for redemption, 2) Approved methods for fiat disbursement, 3) Maximum processing timeframes (e.g., T+2 business days), 4) Fee structure (if any), and 5) Contingency plans for high-volume redemption events.

### Gap 3: GAP-005
**Requirement:** Issuers must have comprehensive risk management frameworks covering operational, cybersecurity, liquidity, and market risks.

**Recommendation:** Develop an Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) Framework. Key components should include: 1) A dedicated Risk Committee, 2) Regular risk assessments (cyber, operational, liquidity), 3) A tested Incident Response and Business Continuity Plan, 4) Specific liquidity risk management for the reserve portfolio.
