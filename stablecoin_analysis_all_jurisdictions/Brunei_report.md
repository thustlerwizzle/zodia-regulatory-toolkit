**Requirement:** Reserve Management and Full Backing (common requirement for stablecoins globally)

**Current Status:** No documented policy found

**Gap:** No policies governing the custody, composition, audit, or segregation of reserve assets backing the stablecoin.

**Requirement:** Anti-Money Laundering/Counter-Financing of Terrorism (AML/CFT) compliance (required under Brunei's AMLCFT Order, 2010 and FATF standards)

**Current Status:** No documented policy found

**Gap:** No AML/CFT program tailored to virtual asset services, including stablecoin transactions. Missing Customer Due Diligence (CDD), transaction monitoring, suspicious activity reporting (SAR), and record-keeping procedures.

**Impact:** Severe regulatory penalties, criminal liability for the company and directors, loss of banking relationships, and inclusion on international watchlists.

**Recommendation:** Implement a robust, risk-based AML/CFT Policy specific to digital assets. Include KYC/CDD procedures for all users, transaction monitoring for unusual patterns, a process for filing SARs with AMBD's Financial Intelligence Unit, and mandatory staff training.
