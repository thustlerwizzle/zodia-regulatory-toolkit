### Gap 1: GAP-001
**Requirement:** Registration/Licensing for stablecoin issuers under EU MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets) Regulation

**Current Status:** No documented policy for stablecoin issuance or regulatory registration

**Gap:** Company lacks policies addressing the mandatory EU authorization/licensing requirements for stablecoin issuers. MiCA requires issuers of significant asset-referenced tokens (ARTs) and e-money tokens (EMTs) to obtain authorization from competent authorities.

**Impact:** Operating without proper authorization could result in regulatory enforcement actions, significant fines (up to 10% of annual turnover under MiCA), cessation of operations, and reputational damage.

**Recommendation:** 1. Immediately assess whether company activities fall under MiCA's scope for stablecoin issuance. 2. ### Gap 2: GAP-002
**Requirement:** Reserve asset requirements and custody arrangements for stablecoins

**Current Status:** No documented reserve management or custody policies

**Gap:** MiCA mandates strict requirements for reserve assets backing stablecoins, including composition, segregation, custody, and regular independent audits. No policies exist to ensure reserve assets are held in secure custody with reputable institutions.

**Impact:** Failure to properly manage reserve assets could lead to insufficient backing for stablecoins, loss of customer funds, regulatory sanctions, and loss of market confidence triggering a run on the stablecoin.

**Recommendation:** 1. Develop a comprehensive reserve management policy specifying eligible assets, diversification requirements, and custody arrangements. 2. Establish procedures for daily valuation and monthly reconciliation of reserves. 3. Implement arrangements for independent reserve audits at least annually.
